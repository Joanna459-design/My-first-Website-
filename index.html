About Me

I'm Joanna — a student from Nasarawa State, Nigeria, who picked up a crochet hook less than a year ago and never looked back. I make everything for myself, piece by piece building a wardrobe that is entirely handmade.

I also use AI as a daily tool for budgeting, cooking, writing, and learning — because creativity and technology go hand in hand.
